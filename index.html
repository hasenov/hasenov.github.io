    Workflow

    Every stage is meticulously planned — from strategy through launch. You’ll always know exactly what phase the project is in.

    Analysis & Briefing

    I gather all the essentials: business goals, key functionalities, audience profile, and market specifics. Then I craft a precise specification and roadmap.

    Architecture & Prototyping

    I design the site’s structure and main user flows, creating a clickable prototype so you can immediately see navigation logic and content placement.

    Visual Design

    I develop a clean, memorable design—selecting fonts and accent colors, and establishing visual hierarchy. All revisions are made until you’re completely satisfied.

    Development & CMS Integration

    I write clean code using WordPress with Bricks Builder, configure the admin area for seamless content management, and integrate contact forms.

    Testing & Optimization

    I verify functionality and appearance across all devices and major browsers, optimize page load speeds, fix bugs, and set up basic SEO parameters.

    Deployment & Support

    I launch your site on your hosting, configure SSL and automated backups, and provide rapid technical support while monitoring stability.
