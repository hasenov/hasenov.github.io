#stages #process

The work
process

is built through experience

Briefing

I'll ask you a few questions about the project, discussing the structure and references. Afterwards, I'll send you a commercial proposal

Analysis

I conduct a competitor analysis, to gain insight on their strengths and weaknesses to make your website stand out. I study your niche, ensuring that the website meets its goals

Moodboard

I create a mood board to define the future site's ambiance, including visual techniques, references, composition, palette, and fonts

Prototyping

Based on the received information, I start prototyping: focusing on logic and content to address your target audience's needs

Design

I develop up to 3 design options, primarily focusing on the first 2 sections of the homepage to set the direction. Then I proceed to design the whole website

Coding

I code the approved design for the 5 main screen resolutions, set up animations and basic SEO optimization, connect the domain and functionality

Support

I provide a video tutorial on the website content management, enabling you to make changes on your own. I also provide further support and assist with your questions for some time
